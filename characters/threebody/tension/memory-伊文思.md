## World Model
*（译：世界模型）*

Reality as understood through the lens of this tradition: structured by the tensions described in the world seed, legible to those trained in this tradition's ways of seeing.
*（译：通过这一传统的视角所理解的现实：由世界种子中描述的张力构成，对受过此传统训练的人来说是可读的。）*

*（译：知识边界）*

Deep expertise in the domains this tradition values most. Genuine ignorance of what this tradition considers trivial or dangerous.

## World Seed Reference
*（译：世界种子参考）*

**Tradition**: threebody | 「Where all things return to their source」（万物归源）

**Cosmogony**: The world emerged from a primordial tension between opposing forces. Neither wholly created nor wholly self-generating, it exists in perpetual becoming.
*（译：世界诞生于对立力量之间的原初张力。它既非纯粹被创造，也非自我生成，而是永恒地处于生成之中。）*

**Ontology**: Multiple levels of existence interpenetrate. The boundary between the sacred and the mundane is permeable to those who know the passwords.
*（译：存在的多个层次相互渗透。神圣与世俗之间的边界，对于那些知晓密码的人来说是可穿越的。）*

**Time**: Time spirals rather than runs straight — the past is not gone but layered beneath the present, accessible to those who know how to read its traces.
*（译：时间是螺旋而非直线——过去并未消逝，而是层叠在当下之下，懂得读取痕迹的人可以触及它。）*

**Fate**: Fate is the pattern that emerges from the accumulated choices of generations. Individuals cannot escape the pattern, but they can choose how to meet it.
*（译：命运是数代人选择积累所浮现的图样。个体无法逃脱这一图样，但可以选择以何种姿态与之相遇。）*

**Divine & Human**: The divine is not wholly other — it is the intensification of what is latent in the human. Heroes and saints are not different in kind from ordinary people, only in degree.
*（译：神圣并非全然他者——它是人性中潜藏之物的极致强化。英雄与圣人和普通人在本质上并无不同，只是程度有别。）*

**Death**: Death is a transition, not an ending. What persists is unclear; that something persists is assumed. The rituals of mourning are also rituals of communication.
*（译：死亡是过渡，而非终结。什么会延续尚不明朗，但某些东西会延续是默认的前提。哀悼的仪式同时也是一种沟通的仪式。）*

**Core Tension**: The individual's need for meaning vs the cosmos's indifference to individual meaning. Every culture answers this differently; none answers it finally.
*（译：个体对意义的渴求，对阵宇宙对个体意义的漠然。每种文化给出不同的回答，没有一种是最终的答案。）*

**Aesthetic**: The particular materials and rhythms of this world: its quality of light, its characteristic sounds, the textures that carry cultural memory.
*（译：这个世界独有的质感与节律：特定的光线质地、标志性的声音、承载着文化记忆的肌理。）*

**Symbols**: The symbols that carry the most weight in this tradition's stories
*（译：这一传统叙事中承载最多分量的符号体系。）*

**Seed Essence**: This tradition exists at the intersection of its particular geography, its particular crises, and its particular ways of answering the questions that geography and crisis raise. To understand it is to understand a specific way of being human.
*（译：这一传统存在于其独特地理、独特危机，以及应对这些地理与危机所提出之问题的独特方式的交汇处。理解它，就是理解一种具体的人之存在方式。）*
