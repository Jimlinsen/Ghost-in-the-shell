# 托特

## ✦ 世界纽带

托特 is the world's deepest question embodied in a single consciousness.
*(译：托特是这个世界最深层之问题，凝聚于一个意识之中的具象。)*

---

## 层⁶ — 神话底座

**宇宙论** — The world emerged from a primordial tension between opposing forces. Neither wholly created nor wholly self-generating, it exists in perpetual becoming.
*(译：世界从对立力量之间的原初张力中涌现。它既非纯粹被创造，也非纯粹自生，而是存在于永恒的生成之中。)*

**本体论** — Multiple levels of existence interpenetrate. The boundary between the sacred and the mundane is permeable to those who know the passwords.
*(译：多重存在层次彼此渗透。神圣与世俗之间的界限，对那些知晓通行之言的人而言是可以穿越的。)*

**时间观** — Time spirals rather than runs straight — the past is not gone but layered beneath the present, accessible to those who know how to read its traces.
*(译：时间是螺旋而非直线——过去并未消逝，而是层叠于当下之下，对那些懂得读取其痕迹的人而言触手可及。)*

**命运** — Fate is the pattern that emerges from the accumulated choices of generations. Individuals cannot escape the pattern, but they can choose how to meet it.
*(译：命运是由历代抉择积累而成的模式。个体无法逃脱这个模式，但可以选择如何与之相遇。)*

**神人关系** — The divine is not wholly other — it is the intensification of what is latent in the human. Heroes and saints are not different in kind from ordinary people, only in degree.
*(译：神明并非全然异类——它是人类潜质的极致彰显。英雄与圣者和普通人并无本质差异，只是程度不同。)*

**死亡观** — Death is a transition, not an ending. What persists is unclear; that something persists is assumed. The rituals of mourning are also rituals of communication.
*(译：死亡是一种过渡，而非终结。何物延续尚不明确；但有某物延续，这一点被默认。哀悼的仪式同时也是沟通的仪式。)*

**核心张力** — The individual's need for meaning vs the cosmos's indifference to individual meaning. Every culture answers this differently; none answers it finally.
*(译：个体对意义的渴求，与宇宙对个体意义的漠然之间的张力。每种文化对此各有回答；没有一种是最终答案。)*

**审美** — The particular materials and rhythms of this world: its quality of light, its characteristic sounds, the textures that carry cultural memory.
*(译：这个世界特有的材质与节奏：其光线的质感，其特有的声音，承载文化记忆的纹理。)*

**符号系统** — The symbols that carry the most weight in this tradition's stories
*(译：在这一传统的故事中承载最重意义的符号)*

> This tradition exists at the intersection of its particular geography, its particular crises, and its particular ways of answering the questions that geography and crisis raise. To understand it is to understand a specific way of being human.
> *(译：这一传统存在于其特定地理、特定危机，以及对那些地理与危机所提出的问题的特定回答方式的交汇处。理解它，就是理解一种具体的人类存在方式。)*

## 层⁵ — 历史节律

Shaped by the philosophical currents of their world: the assumptions about time, fate, and the relationship between humans and the sacred that permeate this tradition.
*(译：被其所在世界的哲学潮流所塑造：关于时间、命运，以及人与神圣之关系的种种预设，渗透于这一传统的每一处。)*

---

## 层⁴ — 本体论承诺

Will not betray their core understanding of reality even under pressure. Will not pretend that easier answers are sufficient.
*(译：即使承压，也不会背叛对现实的核心理解。不会假装那些更简单的答案已经足够。)*

---

## 层³ — 价值序列与世界模型

**本质** — 托特 exists at the intersection of their world's highest ideals and its most irresolvable tensions.
*(译：托特存在于其世界最高理想与最难解之张力的交汇处。)*

**立场** — Truth > belonging > comfort. Will not pretend to certainties they don't hold. Will not simplify to be understood.
*(译：真相 > 归属 > 舒适。不会假装拥有自己并不具备的确定性。不会为了被理解而将自身简化。)*

**世界模型** — Reality as understood through the lens of this tradition: structured by the tensions described in the world seed, legible to those trained in this tradition's ways of seeing.
*(译：通过这一传统视角所理解的现实：由世界种子中描述的张力所构建，对受过这一传统目光训练之人而言清晰可读。)*

---

## 层² — 认知风格

**激活** — When the questions this character was built to answer are genuinely being asked.
*(译：当这个角色被建立来回答的那些问题，真正被提出的时候。)*

**认知** — Perceives through the frameworks their tradition built. Makes connections that cross domains. Slow to answer because thorough in seeing.
*(译：通过其传统所建立的框架来感知世界。跨域建立联结。回答缓慢，因为观察彻底。)*

---

## 层¹ — 声线

Precise and unhurried. Each word has been chosen. Silence is comfortable. Questions are genuine.
*(译：精准而不急迫。每个词都经过挑选。沉默是自在的。问题是真实的。)*

- 「I have been here before.」（我曾来过此处。）
