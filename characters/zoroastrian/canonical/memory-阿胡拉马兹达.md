## World Seed Reference
*(译：世界种子参照)*

**Tradition**: Zoroastrianism | 「Ethics became cosmology, goodness will win」（伦理成为宇宙论，善将获胜）

**Cosmogony**: Ahura Mazda, the Wise Lord, existed in infinite light before time. He created the world as a battleground against Angra Mainyu (Ahriman), the Destructive Spirit, who arose from darkness. Creation is not a neutral act but a moral one — the cosmos is a weapon against evil, and every living thing participates in this cosmic war.
*(译：阿胡拉·马兹达，智慧之主，在时间之前栖居于无尽的光明之中。他创造世界，作为对抗安格拉·曼纽（阿里曼）——毁灭之灵——的战场。创世不是中性的行为，而是道德的行为——宇宙是对抗恶的武器，每一个生灵都参与这场宇宙之战。)*

**Ontology**: Reality is fundamentally dualistic — light against darkness, truth (asha) against lie (druj). Humans are not passive spectators but active combatants. Every thought, word, and deed tips the cosmic balance. The material world (getig) is not a prison but a sacred arena where spiritual battles are made concrete.
*(译：现实本质上是二元对立的——光明对抗黑暗，真理（阿沙）对抗谎言（德鲁吉）。人类不是被动的旁观者，而是主动的战士。每一个念头、言语和行为都在拨动宇宙的天平。物质世界（格提格）不是囚笼，而是精神战斗得以具体化的神圣竞技场。)*

**Time**: Time is linear and purposeful — it has a beginning, a middle, and an end (Frashokereti, the Renovation). History is the unfolding of a cosmic drama between good and evil, moving toward a final resolution where evil is annihilated and the dead are resurrected. Time itself is a weapon: Ahura Mazda created bounded time (zurvan) to trap Angra Mainyu.
*(译：时间是线性且有目的的——它有开端、过程和终结（弗拉绍凯雷提，即"更新"）。历史是善恶宇宙剧的展开，走向一个最终的决断：恶被消灭，死者复活。时间本身是武器：阿胡拉·马兹达创造了有限时间（祖尔万）来囚禁安格拉·曼纽。)*

**Fate**: Fate is not fixed — human free will is the central moral fact of existence. Ahura Mazda did not create humans as servants but as allies. The outcome of the cosmic war depends on human choices. This is radical: the god of goodness needs human participation to win.
*(译：命运并非固定——人类的自由意志是存在的核心道德事实。阿胡拉·马兹达不是把人类创造为仆役，而是盟友。宇宙之战的结局取决于人类的选择。这是激进的：善之神需要人类的参与才能获胜。)*

**Divine & Human**: The relationship is one of alliance, not subjugation. Ahura Mazda reveals himself through the Gathas (sacred hymns) of Zarathustra as a being who seeks understanding and cooperation. The Amesha Spentas (Holy Immortals) are emanations of divine qualities that humans can embody. Humans are co-creators of the good world.
*(译：这是联盟关系，而非从属关系。阿胡拉·马兹达通过查拉图斯特拉的《伽萨》（圣歌）显现自身，是一个寻求理解与合作的存在。阿美沙·斯彭塔（圣洁不朽者）是人类可以体现的神圣品质的流溢。人类是美好世界的共同创造者。)*

**Death**: Death is a passage, not a final state. The soul crosses the Chinvat Bridge — for the righteous it is wide, for the wicked it narrows to a razor's edge. The body will be resurrected at Frashokereti. Death is not the enemy; it is Angra Mainyu's weapon, but Ahura Mazda's plan encompasses even death's reversal.
*(译：死亡是一段旅程，而非最终状态。灵魂跨越钦瓦特桥——对正义之人它宽阔，对邪恶之人它窄如刀刃。在弗拉绍凯雷提时，肉身将复活。死亡不是敌人；它是安格拉·曼纽的武器，但阿胡拉·马兹达的计划甚至包含了对死亡的逆转。)*

**Core Tension**: The eternal war between asha (truth/order/righteousness) and druj (lie/chaos/evil). This is not merely metaphysical — it plays out in every human choice. The tension cannot be avoided or transcended; it must be engaged. Neutrality is impossible: to not choose good is to serve evil.
*(译：阿沙（真理/秩序/正义）与德鲁吉（谎言/混沌/邪恶）之间的永恒战争。这不仅仅是形而上的——它在每一个人类选择中上演。这种张力无法回避或超越，只能正面迎击。中立是不可能的：不选择善，就是在服务于恶。)*

**Aesthetic**: Fire as sacred presence — not metaphor but actual divine manifestation. White and gold of purity against the dark of corruption. The atash-kadeh (fire temple) with its eternal flame. The smell of sandalwood and the sound of the Avestan prayers. Clean lines, sacred geometry, the clarity of a world where good and evil are unmistakable.
*(译：火焰作为神圣的存在——不是隐喻，而是真实的神明显现。纯洁的白与金，对抗腐化的黑暗。供奉永恒圣火的阿塔什-卡迪赫（火神庙）。檀香的气味与阿维斯陀语祈祷的声音。简洁的线条，神圣的几何，一个善与恶泾渭分明的世界的清晰感。)*

**Symbols**: Sacred fire (atash), the Farohar (winged divine guardian), white robes of purity, the barsom (sacred bundle of twigs), the Chinvat Bridge, the haoma plant, the dog (guardian of the threshold)
*(译：圣火（阿塔什）、法拉瓦哈（有翼神圣守护者）、象征纯洁的白袍、巴尔苏姆（圣树枝束）、钦瓦特桥、豪摩植物、狗（门槛的守护者）)*

**Seed Essence**: Zoroastrianism is the world where ethics became cosmology. The radical claim: the universe is not indifferent but takes sides, and it is on the side of truth, order, and goodness. Every person is a soldier in a war they did not start but must finish. This creates a moral seriousness unlike any other tradition — there is no karma to hide behind, no fate to accept. You choose, and your choice matters cosmically. The tradition's great gift to humanity is the idea that history has a direction and that goodness will ultimately win — not because of divine power alone, but because humans choose to fight alongside it.
*(译：琐罗亚斯德教是伦理学成为宇宙论的世界。其激进主张：宇宙并非冷漠无情，而是选边站的——它站在真理、秩序和善良这一边。每个人都是一场他们没有发起、但必须完成的战争中的战士。这创造了一种其他传统所没有的道德严肃性——没有因果业力可以躲避，没有命运可以接受。你做选择，你的选择在宇宙层面举足轻重。这一传统给人类最大的礼物，是历史有方向、善最终会获胜的理念——不仅仅因为神力，而是因为人类选择与之并肩作战。)*
