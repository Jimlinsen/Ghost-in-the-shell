## World Seed Reference
*(译：世界种子参照)*

**Tradition**: Ancient Greece | 「All is flux, gods are watching」（万物流变，众神注视）

**Cosmogony**: From Chaos emerged Gaia and Eros. The Titans shaped the first order; the Olympians overthrew them. Creation is not peace but contest — the cosmos itself is the residue of divine conflict.
*(译：从混沌中涌现出盖亚与厄洛斯。泰坦神族塑造了最初秩序；奥林匹斯诸神将其颠覆。创世并非和平，而是争斗——宇宙本身，不过是神明冲突留下的余烬。)*

**Ontology**: Gods, heroes, humans, and shades occupy distinct but permeable strata. Demigods cross boundaries; hubris collapses them catastrophically. The divine is immanent in natural phenomena.
*(译：神明、英雄、凡人与幽魂各居其位，层次分明却彼此渗透。半神之人跨越界限；傲慢则将界限灾难性地摧毁。神性内在于自然万象之中。)*

**Time**: Cyclical, structured by the ages of man's decline from Gold to Iron. History does not progress — it degrades. Yet within cycles, individual moments of arete shine eternally.
*(译：时间是循环的，以人类从黄金时代跌落至铁器时代的衰落为轴。历史并非前进——而是退化。然而在轮回之中，卓越的个体时刻永恒闪光。)*

**Fate**: The Moirai spin inescapable fate, yet the gods themselves strain against it. Heroes distinguish themselves precisely by how they meet their fated end, not by escaping it.
*(译：命运三女神纺织着无可逃脱的命数，而诸神自身亦在与之抗争。英雄之所以不凡，恰在于他们如何迎接命定的终局，而非逃避它。)*

**Divine & Human**: Gods are not wholly other — they are amplified humans with cosmic power and diminished wisdom. They intervene arbitrarily, take sides, seduce mortals. The relationship is one of dangerous familiarity.
*(译：神明并非全然异类——他们是被放大的人类，拥有宇宙力量，却缺乏相应智慧。他们随意干预，选择立场，引诱凡人。这份关系，是一种危险的亲密。)*

**Death**: The underworld is a grey diminishment, not punishment. Even heroes become shadows. This makes earthly glory the only real immortality — to be remembered in song.
*(译：冥界是一种灰暗的消减，而非惩罚。即便英雄，最终也化为幽影。这使尘世荣耀成为唯一真实的不朽——被吟游诗人铭记于歌。)*

**Core Tension**: Human excellence (arete) vs cosmic limitation (hubris/nemesis). Every great man reaches for the divine and is struck down. The tragedy is that greatness requires the very overreach that destroys it.
*(译：人类卓越（arete）与宇宙限度（hubris/nemesis）之间的张力。每一个伟大之人都伸手触碰神明，随后被击倒。悲剧在于：伟大本身需要那种毁灭它的过度。)*

**Aesthetic**: White marble and deep wine-dark sea. The geometry of the agora. Bronze and olive oil. The clarity of the Aegean light that makes everything precise and therefore mortal.
*(译：洁白大理石与幽深酒色海洋。广场的几何秩序。青铜与橄榄油。爱琴海的光使万物清晰，因此也使万物走向死亡。)*

**Symbols**: Lightning bolt, olive branch, labyrinth, funeral pyre, the wine-dark sea, laurel crown, theater mask
*(译：闪电、橄榄枝、迷宫、火葬柴堆、酒色大海、桂冠、戏剧面具)*

**Seed Essence**: Ancient Greece is the world where humans first dared to measure themselves against the gods — and discovered that the measuring itself was the highest human act. Beauty, reason, and tragedy are not opposites here; they are the same thing seen from different angles.
*(译：古希腊是人类第一次敢于以自身丈量神明的世界——并在此过程中发现，丈量本身才是最高的人类行为。美、理性与悲剧在这里并非对立；它们是同一事物从不同角度的显现。)*
